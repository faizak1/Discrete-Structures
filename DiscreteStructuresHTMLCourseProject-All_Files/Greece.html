Angelica's Favorite Greek Desserts



Try These Delicious Desserts for a Taste of Greece!


Baklava
  • Description: A rich Greek pastry consisting of sheets of phyllo dough layered with chopped walnuts, butter, and cinnamon, baked, and soaked in a honey and sugar syrup
  • Difficulty Level: Medium
  • Link to Recipe: Baklava
Galaktoboureko
  • Description: Galaktoboureko is a traditional Greek dessert made with layers of golden brown crispy phyllo dough, sprinkled with melted butter, filled with the most creamy custard and bathed in lemon and cinnamon scented syrup. A perfect after dinner dessert. Can be served cold or warm with cinnamon sprinkled on top.
  • Difficulty Level: Hard
  • Link to Recipe: Galaktoboureko
Koulourakia
  • Description: A delicious and simple cookie perfect for any time of the year. Many people love to dunk this cookie in morning coffee.
  • Difficulty Level: Easy
  • Link to Recipe: Koulourakia
Melomakarona
  • Description: This delectable cookie soaked in honey and topped with walnuts is perfect for Christmas dessert.
  • Difficulty Level: Easy
  • Link to Recipe: Melomakarona
